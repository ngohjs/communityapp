Community App — Visual Design Systems

Five contrasting visual directions derived from the Community App PRD, tailored for sales leaders, influencers, and reps collaborating on content. Each block illustrates color, type, layout, signature elements, and motion principles.

01 · Pipeline Pulse — Momentum Through Clarity

Energetic CRM-inspired UI emphasizing speed and laser focus. Built for sales teams racing through content catalogs with no ambiguity.

Electric Indigo #5451FF
Signal Coral #FF6B5A
Arctic Mist #F4F7FB
Graphite #24273A

Headlines — General Sans Bold 700

Accelerate pipeline clarity

Body/UI — Inter Regular 400

Library cards use Inter 600 for labels, 400 for summaries, maintaining crisp readability.

Spacing: 8 · 16 · 24 · 32
Grid: 12-col / 96px margins
Card Width: 4-col increments
Signature Element: Oblique “momentum bar” — an angled gradient slab that slides behind featured content to indicate progress and urgency.
Motion Principles:
  • Fast transitions (120ms ease-out) mirroring decisive sales actions.
  • Horizontal carousels shift 12px with slight overshoot.
  • Hover micro-motions scale CTAs to 1.03 for tactile feedback.

02 · Field Notes — Analog Warmth for Digital Peers

Feels like a trusted field journal. Friendly for reps capturing insights, with tactile visuals and calm typography.

Kraft Tan #D9B68D
Blueprint Navy #1F3A60
Highlighter Mint #9EE7D7
Soft Charcoal #3C3C3C

Headlines — Recoleta Bold 700

Curated playbooks, handwritten warmth

Body/UI — IBM Plex Sans 400/500

Structured labels feel like notebook annotations, balancing legibility and personality.

Spacing: 6 · 12 · 18 · 24
Grid: 10-col / 84px gutters
Mobile: Fluid stacked cards
Signature Element: “Tabbed notebook edge” — dashed dividers and corner tabs create the feel of a physical journal.
Motion Principles:
  • Gentle 180ms ease-in-out transitions mimicking page flips.
  • Tab changes include 2° tilt and shadow shift.
  • Highlight sweeps animate like scribbled highlighter strokes.

03 · Aurora Canvas — Guided Discovery via Light

Sweeping gradients and airy layouts invite exploration, ideal for leaders sifting through curated decks.

Aurora Violet #7F5AF0
Dawn Peach #FFB3A7
Polar White #FCFCFF
Midnight Ink #2A2D3A

Headlines — Satoshi Light/Bold Combo

Illuminated resource discovery

Body/UI — Work Sans 400/500

Friendly geometric sans keeps the experience soft while remaining highly legible.

Spacing: 12 · 24 · 36
Grid: 16-col fluid / 80px gutters
Hero overlay uses parallax
Signature Element: “Aurora arc” — flowing gradient ribbons weaving behind hero content, subtly shifting hue as the user scrolls.
Motion Principles:
  • Parallax gradients at 0.75x scroll speed.
  • Cards fade-up 20px over 160ms (ease-in-out).
  • Hover reveals luminous edge glow expanding outward.

04 · Terra Trust — Grounded Reliability for Enterprise Leaders

Earthy, data-driven aesthetic instilling confidence for senior stakeholders reviewing strategic assets.

Terra Clay #B46A55
Boardroom Slate #2E3B45
Verdant Accent #6AA97F
Outlook Mist #E6ECF1

Headlines — Source Serif Pro SemiBold 600

Executive insights, grounded

Body/UI — Roboto 400/500

Roboto ensures crisp readability for dense metrics and audit trails.

Spacing: 10 · 20 · 30
Grid: 12-col / 112px margins
Cards: 30px padding, 20px radius
Signature Element: “Performance ledger” — subtle horizontal rules and embossed badges on cards reminiscent of premium reports.
Motion Principles:
  • Measured 200ms ease-out transitions; no flashy easing.
  • KPI counters animate from 0 to value in 600ms.
  • Modals slide up 12px with light shadow expansion.

05 · Quantum Loop — Modular Network for Agile Teams

Futuristic node-based UI illustrating knowledge moving through the community, ideal for fast-moving reps.

Neon Azure #2AD2FF
Cosmic Graphite #151820
Signal Lime #A6FF00
Soft Steel #3F4B5C

Headlines — Space Grotesk Bold 700

Dynamic knowledge mesh

Body/UI — Manrope 400/600

Rounded geometry keeps the sci-fi visual language human and approachable.

Spacing: 4 · 8 · 16 · 32
Grid: 18-col modular
Nodes snap to dotted matrix
Signature Element: Animated node mesh overlay pulsing subtly to represent collaboration pathways between members and content artifacts.
Motion Principles:
  • Spring physics (stiffness 150) for card reveals.
  • Hover shifts 8px with 100ms delay, plus neon glow.
  • Context panels unfold from nodes with scale/opacity combo.
